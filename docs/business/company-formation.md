# Company Formation Guide — Rennet Systems

## Step 1: Choose Your Company Name

- Check name availability: `npm run screen -- "Your Company Name"`
- Avoid sensitive words (Royal, Bank, Trust, etc.) without approval
- Name must end in "Ltd" or "Limited"

## Step 2: Recommended SIC Codes

| Code | Description | Use |
|------|-------------|-----|
| 62012 | Business and domestic software development | Primary — website builds |
| 58290 | Other software publishing | Secondary — Skillio app |
| 62090 | Other information technology service activities | Backup |

## Step 3: Register at Companies House

- Go to: https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk/
- Cost: £50 online
- Processing time: ~24 hours
- You'll need: Name, SIC code, registered address, director details, PSC details, share structure
- Recommended share structure: 100 ordinary shares at £1 each, held by you

## Step 4: After Registration

1. Corporation Tax — auto-linked from CH registration
2. Self Assessment — register at gov.uk as a director
3. PAYE — register as an employer to pay your salary
4. Business bank account — Tide/Starling/Monzo (free tier)
5. ICO registration — £40/yr at ico.org.uk
6. D-U-N-S Number — free at dnb.com (required for Apple Developer enrollment)

## Step 5: Insurance

Professional Indemnity Insurance is essential before taking on clients.
- Estimated cost: ~£200-400/yr
- Providers: Hiscox, Simply Business, PolicyGenius
- Cover: £250k-£500k typical for solo developer
